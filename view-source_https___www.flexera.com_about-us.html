Our Proud History

A history of transforming IT

Since day one, Flexera has sought to be an essential IT optimization resource, from developing one of the most widely used tools for software packaging (InstallShield) more than 30 years ago to acquiring the market leader in cloud management (RightScale) 

Chaos Genius, 2026

Flexera acquires ProsperOps and Chaos Genius to deliver the most comprehensive, intelligent and autonomous FinOps solution.

Spot, 2025

Flexera acquires Spot to bolster its multi-cloud cost management, continuous optimization and FinOps capabilities.

Flexera One, 2020

Launch of Flexera One, our unified solution that integrates all our capabilities into a single user interface.

Snow, 2024

Flexera acquires Snow Software. Snow solutions are added to the Flexera portfolio providing unrivaled Technology Intelligence.

RISC Networks, 2019

Flexera strengthens its presence in cloud management by acquiring RISC Networks and adding Foundation and CloudScape to its list of integrated Solutions

Secunia, 2015

Flexera acquires Denmark-based Secunia, a leader in vulnerability intelligence and remediation solutions backed by a dedicated threat intelligence research team.

RightScale, 2018

Cloud Management Platform and Optima from RightScale, along with SaaS Manager, are brought into Flexera's portfolio of solutions. Flexera is named a leader in cloud management platforms by Gartner.

Secunia, 2015

The products that would become Software Vulnerability Manager and Software Vulnerability Research are added to Flexera's solution portfolio.

BDNA, 2017

Flexera acquires BDNA and, with it, Technopedia, the world's largest enriched technology catalog of IT asset data. This forms the backbone of the Technology Intelligence Platform.

2014

Flexera moves its headquarters to our current offices in Itasca, IL.

2012

App Portal (now App Broker) is added to Flexera's line of solutions, giving the ability to provide enterprises with a self-service app store.

Flexera, 2008-2009

Acresso Software spins off from Macrovision as a private company, becoming Flexera Software in 2009.

ManageSoft, 2010

Flexera opens its first Asia-Pacific office after purchasing ManageSoft.

Macrovision, 2004

Software giant Macrovision acquires InstallShield.

2000

The company opens its first European office.

2000

AdminStudio is developed, strengthening InstallShield's place in the application management space.

1987

InstallShield is born. This is the company's-eventually Flexera's-first major entry into the software market.

1998

FlexNet Manager for Engineering Applications is launched. The FlexNet Manager solutions will become the industry standard for software management.
